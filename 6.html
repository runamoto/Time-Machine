A Love Letter to Village By the Grange
Mundane Archive
Issue 1

I don’t remember my first time at The Village by Grange, but what I do remember is the feeling of being slightly disappointed by it. As an international student, I was expecting something more grand to come along with my twenty four thousand dollar tuition fee. “At least the Popeyes is halal” I said to myself. Little did I know that this little place that we call The Grange would consume so many hours of my time, money and thoughts.

Once upon a time... In 1967, a company named Windlass had big plans. Plans to build something new and exciting called the Village by the Grange, a complex with tall apartment buildings.
